Elevator pitch
GlowSafe is a web app that turns abstract UV data into clear, personalised guidance for young Australians. Instead of generic “slip, slop, slap” messages, it builds a quick skin and lifestyle profile, pulls live UV and weather for your area, and translates it into plain‑language actions: when UV is risky (even on cloudy days), how to protect your skin, and what to do next. The experience feels more like checking the weather than reading a health brochure—fast, visual, and judgement‑free—so staying sun‑smart becomes part of your everyday routine rather than an occasional warning.
